    Overview of all dog breeds

    In All Best Dogs we stand for one thing above all: the great declaration of love for the dog as man's best friend. We love all dogs and that means all breeds, and mixed breeds equally.

    You will find here all dog breeds, from A to Z. In the breed descriptions you will find everything about the character, temperament as well as the size, weight and average life expectancy. The description of what the breed stands for in use and what the dog's bred group is, will help you to find the perfect dog for you.
